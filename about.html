About Hubris Engineers

Measuring the world, engineering its future.

With over a decade of hands-on experience, we combine field surveying, digital engineering and ELV-ICT integration under one roof — delivering reliable, accountable outcomes for India's most demanding projects.

Our story

A decade of precision engineering

Hubris Engineers was built on a simple belief: accurate data is the foundation of every successful project. From topographic and construction surveys to high-precision 3D laser scanning, we help clients understand exactly what exists before they build, retrofit or automate.

Today we operate across three integrated divisions — Surveying, 3D Modeling & Digital Engineering, and ELV-ICT Solutions — supported by ISO-aligned calibration through our associated unit, Vertex Calibration. This lets clients work with a single accountable partner from the first measurement to the final smart-building handover.
